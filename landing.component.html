About Us

We at E-Cell IIT Bombay, through this initiative, aim to bridge the gap between students and startups. FInCoF(Freelancers, Interns and CoFounders) connects startups across the country with the students of IIT Bombay. Each startup listed on this portal offers a role in their company, for students with the same vision and passion.
This platform with over 100+ startups onboard offers students the opportunity to interact with the ever-growing startup ecosystem and for the startups to benefit from the expertise and skills of the students.
